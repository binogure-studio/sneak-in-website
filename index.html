It's time for a new adventure.

Sneak In is one of the most challenging and addictive marbles shooter game you will ever play.

Shoot balls to make sets of three marbles and to trigger chain reaction and combos to stop the greedy snake from eating all of your marbles.

Play through colorful and challenging levels to make your way to the top of the scoreboard with your friends.
